About Us

At Agrovision Crop Science, we are committed to revolutionizing Indian agriculture by delivering high-quality, research-backed solutions that empower farmers to increase productivity and sustainability. As a trusted manufacturer of Plant Growth Regulators (PGRs) and micronutrients, we bring innovation, experience, and reliability to every field we touch. Founded with a vision to support farmers with effective and affordable agri-inputs, our company combines scientific expertise with a deep understanding of crop needs. Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities and strict quality control ensure that every product we deliver meets the highest standards of performance and safety. With a dedicated team of agronomists, researchers, and field experts, we continue to develop advanced formulations tailored for Indian soil and climatic conditions. Our goal is to help farmers achieve better yields, healthier crops, and long-term soil fertility.
